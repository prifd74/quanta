Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Learn more about how Quanta stays free and awesome.

Is Quanta really 100% free forever?

Yes! Quanta is completely free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or subscription plans. We believe in providing powerful Discord bot features to everyone without any financial barriers. Our mission is to keep it free forever.

How do you keep the bot free? What's the catch?

There's no catch! We're passionate developers who believe in community-driven projects. We maintain Quanta through optional donations from users who love the bot, and we keep our infrastructure costs low through efficient coding and smart resource management.

Are there any usage limits or restrictions?

Nope! All features are available to all users without any limits. You can use music commands, moderation tools, AI features, and everything else as much as you want. No daily limits, no monthly quotas, no restrictions.

What makes Quanta different from other Discord bots?

Quanta offers premium-level features completely free. While other bots charge for advanced features like high-quality music, AI integration, or detailed analytics, we provide all of these at no cost. Plus, our focus on user experience and continuous development sets us apart.

How often do you update and add new features?

We release updates regularly! New features are added monthly, bug fixes are deployed weekly, and critical updates happen as needed. We're constantly listening to user feedback and working to improve Quanta based on your suggestions.

Can I contribute to Quanta or suggest features?

Absolutely! Quanta is community-driven, and we love hearing from users. Join our support Discord to suggest features, report bugs, or even contribute code if you're a developer. Your input helps shape Quanta's future!
